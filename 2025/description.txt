Objective:
	This year utilized an exothermic reaction stopping mechanism;
	As the reaction proceeds, the heat in an area increases, which is measured by a thermocouple
	and supplied to the arduino. Once the temperature change is large enough, the arduino
	signals the stop and shuts off the motor.

Notes:
	This car had significant difficulties measuring thermocouple values, 
	which led to difficulty signaling the stop properly. After extensive testing, 
	blocking EM radiation and physically stabilizing the arduino was somewhat helpful.
